mutual


mutual is a human resource platform that connects founders and executive teams in emerging startup ecosystems such as South Asia, South East Asia, MENA, etc. with top technologists and problem-solvers looking to share their knowledge and expertise. Our mission at Mutual is to democratise entrepreneurial knowledge and problem-solve collectively and not have borders come in the way of access to building the best possible company.

We aim to connect work through their trickiest challenges, be it technical infrastructure, team culture, or product vision and strategy by leveraging expertise of people who have done it before. We utilize the SMART goal framework, because we find it gives the best outcomes.



We do a minimum commitment of 3 one hour sessions, because we find that is what is needed to add value. 1/2 payment will be made up front.
